Human Centered Design
That tells a story

Designing with a synergy of user experience, visual interactive graphics, and web production

I love using my creativity to make things beautiful. Beyond satisfying the visual eye; however, beautiful things needs to be helpful and not frustrating to use when it involves people. That is why it is important for me to apply human-centered design in my work and process. "People come first" will always be the foundation I work from when building positive user experiences and designing beautiful user interfaces. With a background in graphic design and developing my own websites, I blend everything together to simply make everything "just work."
